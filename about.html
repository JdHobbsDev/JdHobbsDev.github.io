About Minds Matter

Minds Matter is a Discord bot designed to promote mental health awareness and provide tools for emotional well-being. It was created with a deep passion and commitment to supporting mental health, inspired by personal experiences. Whether you're looking for mental health resources, activities to help de-stress, or just some inspiration, Minds Matter is here for you.

Why Was It Created?

Minds Matter was inspired by loved ones who faced mental health struggles. It’s a small step towards helping others on their journey toward well-being, and it’s meant to be a source of comfort and guidance.

Features of Minds Matter

  • Daily gratitude logs to promote positivity.
  • Mood tracking to help you understand your emotional patterns.
  • Fun mind games to give you a mental break.
  • Helplines and emergency resources for immediate support.
